Count even and odd elements in array

Input Format
----
all the elements of array A

Output Format
----
output 2 lines with 2 integers.

Example 1:
Input: Array = [1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9]
Output: 
	Total even elements: 4
  Total odd elements: 5

Example 2:
Input: Array = [4,5,3,1,2]
Output: 
	Total even elements: 2
  Total odd elements: 3
